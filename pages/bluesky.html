Bluesky

Social Media • Privacy Rating: 4/10

Privacy Rating 4/10

Bluesky is a decentralized social network protocol aiming to give users more control over their data and social graph, though it has faced early-stage challenges.

Pros & Cons

✅
Data Ownership: Users can self-host Personal Data Servers (PDS), truly owning their data and social identity, with options to import/export it.
✅
No Advertisements: The platform is currently ad-free.
✅
Fully Open Source: The underlying AT Protocol and client applications are open source, allowing for public scrutiny and development.
✅
Communication Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving promotional communications.
✅
Data Portability: You can request access to and deletion of your data from a hosted PDS.
❌
Third-Party Cookies: Third-party cookies are used for functionality, but Bluesky provides instructions on how to opt out of them.
❌
Moderation Controversy: Faced controversy regarding its initial policy on hate speech and slurs in user handles, which led to a user "posting strike" and a public apology in 2023. This prompted updates to their ToS and the formation of a trust and safety team.

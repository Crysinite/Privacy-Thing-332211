Snapchat

Messaging • Privacy Rating: 10/10

Privacy Rating 10/10

Snapchat is a popular multimedia messaging app known for its ephemeral content, but its privacy policies and data practices raise several concerns.

Pros & Cons

✅
Ad Opt-Out: You can opt out of some forms of targeted advertising within the app's settings.
✅
Data Portability: You can request access to and deletion of your account data.
❌
Encryption Concerns: Snapchat claims Snaps are end-to-end encrypted, but this has been questioned. The potential for a man-in-the-middle (MITM) attack to decrypt traffic remains a theoretical risk.
❌
Tracking: Snapchat collects significant user data and utilizes tracking via third-party cookies and other technologies.
❌
Government Data Sharing: Personal information may be shared with government or law enforcement agencies without notifying you.
❌
Off-Platform Tracking: Gathers and tracks information about your activities on other websites and services.
❌
Waiver of Moral Rights: You waive your moral rights to your content, giving Snapchat broad freedom to use and manipulate it without requiring your specific permission or attribution.
❌
Data Persistence: "Deleted" content may not be truly deleted and can remain on backup servers.

Yahoo Search

Search Engine • Privacy Rating: 9/10

Privacy Rating 9/10

Yahoo Search is a web search engine that is now primarily powered by Microsoft's Bing, inheriting many of its tracking practices while operating under Yahoo's own privacy policy.

Pros & Cons

✅
Detailed Policy: Information on how Yahoo collects and uses data is detailed in its privacy policy.
✅
No Data Selling Claim: Yahoo claims not to sell any of your personal data directly.
❌
Data Collection & Tracking: Yahoo still collects user data, tracks behavior, and uses device fingerprinting for its own services.
❌
Data for Advertising: Your personal data is used to serve you targeted advertisements.
❌
No Liability for Breaches: Yahoo's terms state it assumes no liability for unauthorized access to your personal information.
❌
Powered by Bing: Because it is powered by Bing, it inherits Microsoft's extensive tracking practices from the search results it provides.
❌
History of Data Breaches: Yahoo has experienced one of the largest data breaches in history, which occurred in 2013 and 2014, compromising billions of accounts.

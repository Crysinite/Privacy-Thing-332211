Google Search

Search Engine • Privacy Rating: 11/10

Privacy Rating 11/10

Google Search is the most visited website of all time, and is currently the most popular website..

Pros & Cons

✅
Data Portability: You can request access to and deletion of your data through Google Takeout.
✅
Ad Controls: You can opt out of targeted advertising, though general data collection continues.
✅
Readable ToS: The Terms of Service are generally written to be easy to understand.
❌
Heavy Data Collection: Employs extremely heavy data collection, user tracking, and device fingerprinting across all its services.
❌
Location Tracking: Your location data is collected, used for profiling, and shared with advertisers.
❌
Referral Tracking: Google tracks which web page referred you to its search engine, adding to your profile.
❌
Speech Restriction: According to Google's terms, users are restricted from expressing negative opinions about the company on its platforms.
❌
Data Retention: May keep a secure, anonymized record of your data for analytical purposes even after the retention period has ended.
❌
Service Throttling: Google may throttle or impose limits on your use of their service without reason.
❌
Sponsored Links & AI: Users dislike the heavy amount of sponsored links and the integration of the Gemini AI, which can blur the line between organic and paid results.
❌
Malicious Ads: Cybercriminals have successfully advertised harmful phishing websites at the top of Google's search results via sponsored links.
❌
Search Result Bias: Faces controversy over allegations that it purposely promotes specific websites to the top of its search results.
❌
Voice & Camera Data: If using voice search or Google Lens, your voice and camera data is collected.

YouTube

Social Media • Privacy Rating: 11/10

Privacy Rating 11/10

Owned by Google, YouTube is the world's largest video-sharing platform and a significant part of Google's extensive data collection ecosystem.

Pros & Cons

✅
Ad Opt-Out: You can opt out of targeted advertisements, although this does not stop data collection.
✅
Free Access: Provides free access to a vast and diverse library of content.
❌
Heavy Data Collection: Employs extremely heavy amounts of data collection, device fingerprinting, and user tracking across services.
❌
No E2E Encryption: All views, comments, and interactions are logged and analyzed, even for users without an account.
❌
Browser History Access: As part of the Google ecosystem, YouTube can access and utilize your broader browser history for profiling.
❌
Data Persistence: YouTube comments and videos are not guaranteed to be permanently deleted and may remain on backup servers indefinitely.
❌
Information Disclosure: Reserves the right to disclose your personal information to third parties or governments without notifying you.
❌
Moderation & Copyright Issues: Faces heavy controversies related to poor moderation, bot activity, and aggressive, often unfair, copyright strikes against creators.

Bing

Search Engine • Privacy Rating: 10/10

Privacy Rating 10/10

Bing is Microsoft's answer to Google Search. It is deeply integrated into the Microsoft ecosystem and leverages user data for advertising and its AI chatbot, Copilot.

Pros & Cons

✅
Data Deletion: You can request to access and delete your personal data via the Microsoft Privacy Dashboard.
✅
Ad Controls: You can opt out of targeted advertising.
✅
GDPR Compliance: Bing claims to be GDPR compliant for its European users.
✅
Transparent Policy: Their Privacy Policy explains in detail what data is collected, used, and shared.
❌
Browser History Access: Bing can access your browsing history, especially when using the Edge browser.
❌
Heavy Data Collection: Employs extremely heavy data collection, user tracking, and device fingerprinting.
❌
Location Tracking: Your location data is collected, used for profiling, and shared.
❌
Data Transfer in Bankruptcy: The privacy policy allows Bing to sell or transfer your personal data as part of a bankruptcy or other financial transaction.
❌
Malicious Ads: There have been instances where Bing has displayed sponsored ads containing links to malicious phishing sites disguised as legitimate downloads.
❌
AI Issues: Its AI chatbot, Copilot, has been known to provide factually incorrect information and exhibit other concerning behaviors.
